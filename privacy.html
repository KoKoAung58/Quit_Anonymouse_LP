Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains what information we collect for Quit Anonymous: Quit Porn and how we use it.

Last updated: 14 November 2025

1. Who we are

Quit Anonymous: Quit Porn ("Quit Anonymous," "we," "us") is an early-stage product helping adults reduce exposure to online porn in a private, non-judgmental way.

Contact us anytime at quitlyapp@gmail.com.

2. What this policy covers

This policy currently covers the public website at https://quitanonymous.app/ and the waitlist form hosted on Netlify. When the product launches, we’ll publish an in-app privacy policy describing on-device data.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Waitlist information

If you join the waitlist we collect your email address (required) and first name (optional). Netlify processes submissions on our behalf. Please avoid sharing sensitive personal details in the free-text fields.

3.2 Technical information

Our hosting provider logs limited technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information to prevent abuse and keep the site secure.

4. How we use information

  • Send essential waitlist and launch updates.
  • Respond when you contact us with questions or feedback.
  • Maintain the security and reliability of the website.

We do not sell or rent your information.

5. Legal bases (for applicable regions)

If you reside in the EEA, UK, or similar jurisdictions, we rely on consent for emails and legitimate interest for site operations.

6. Retention

We retain your waitlist information until we launch or until you unsubscribe/request deletion, whichever comes first. We may retain limited records if required by law.

7. Your choices

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal information by emailing quitlyapp@gmail.com. Every email we send includes a one-click unsubscribe link.

8. Email communications

Expect occasional updates about launch timing, early access, and important policy notices. We do not send daily newsletters.

9. Children

Quit Anonymous is for adults only. If we learn we’ve collected information from a minor, we’ll delete it promptly.

10. Data transfers

Infrastructure may be located outside your country. By using the site you consent to transferring your information to those locations while we maintain reasonable safeguards.

11. Third-party services

We rely on Netlify and other vendors for hosting, email, analytics, or form handling. These partners process data on our behalf and have their own privacy policies.

12. Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information, but no online service can be 100% secure.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We’ll revise the “Last updated” date and, when appropriate, notify the waitlist by email.

14. Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns? Email quitlyapp@gmail.com.
