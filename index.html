Is this for me?

Common questions from people who are curious yet cautious.

How private is it?
There are no public feeds and no real names in groups. You stay anonymous inside a 4-person group and control what you share.
Who is it for?
Adults who want anonymous accountability, 24/7 AI support, and panic mode tools without public sharing.
How does panic mode blocking work?
You select trigger apps and allow Screen Time or family controls. Panic mode blocks access for 20 minutes or until you finish grounding tasks like journaling + breathing, walking, and meditation.
What happens when an urge hits?
You can switch on panic mode, get blocked from trigger apps, and open the 24/7 AI chat or a relapse reflection to reset.
What data do you store?
For now we only keep your waitlist email and optional first name. In the app, data includes check-ins, streaks, relapse entries, panic mode actions, and AI chat to personalize support.
How do I unsubscribe?
Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link. You can also email quitlyapp@gmail.com to be removed instantly.
