Is this for me?

Common questions from people who are curious yet cautious.

How private is it?
There are no public feeds. Screen Time stays on your device and the only outbound alert is the SMS you ask us to send a sponsor when adult content is detected.
Who is it for?
Adults who want AA-style sponsor accountability paired with Screen Time detection and a calm community without spending hours in in-person meetings.
How does blocking work with Apple Screen Time?
You’ll set Screen Time categories, limits, and downtime windows. Quit Anonymous listens for those signals so it can lock things down and text your sponsor in the moment.
What happens when adult content is detected?
We pause access, send your sponsor a discreet SMS, and prompt you to drop a note in the community or complete the grounding exercise you chose.
What data do you store?
For now we only keep your waitlist email and optional first name. Detection logs stay on-device, and sponsor alerts only include timestamp, platform, and the nickname you share.
How do I unsubscribe?
Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link. You can also email quitlyapp@gmail.com to be removed instantly.
